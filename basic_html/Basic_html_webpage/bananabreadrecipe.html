Banana Bread Recipe

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making moist banana bread! This classic treat is perfect for satisfying your cravings with a slice of comfort. Follow this simple recipe to create a delicious loaf that’s soft on the inside, golden on the outside, and packed with rich banana flavor that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas (mashed)
  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ⅓ cup melted butter or oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F) and grease a loaf pan.
  2. In a bowl, mash bananas and mix in egg, melted butter/oil, and vanilla.
  3. Add flour, sugar, baking soda, and salt. Stir gently (don’t overmix).
  4. Pour batter into the pan and bake for 45–55 minutes.
  5. Let cool, slice, and enjoy!
